Pilot a rocket through an asteroid field:

• The rocket is constantly falling down. Rapidly tap the screen to fly upwards.
• Dodge all asteroids (  ) or your rocket will explode.
• Collect as many stars (  ) as possible. Collecting multiple stars in a row gives more points.
• Go through a portal (  ) to complete the level.
• Your score must be higher than the level's goal to pass it.
• To access the next level, you must pass the previous one.
